JavaScript History

  • 1995 JavaScript was invented by Brendan Eich
  • 1996 Netscape and Brendan Eich took JavaScript to the ECMA international standards organization, and a technical committee (TC39) was created to develop the language.
  • June 1997 ECMA-262 Edition 1 was released.
  • August 1998 ECMAScript 2 was released
  • December 1999 ECMAScript 3 was released
  • 2008 ECMAScript 4 was abandond
  • December 2009 ECMAScript 5 was released
  • June 2015 ECMAScript 6 was released
  • June 2016 ECMAScript 7 was released
  • June 2017 ECMAScript 8 was released
  • June 2018 ECMAScript 9 was released
  • June 2019 ECMAScript 10 was released
  • June 2020 ECMAScript 11 was released
  • June 2021 ECMAScript 12 was released
  • June 2022 ECMAScript 13 was released
